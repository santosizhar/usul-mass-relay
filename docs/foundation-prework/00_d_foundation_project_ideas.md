# Foundation Project Ideas

This document lists project concepts built on top of the Foundation.
Each project is primarily a configuration of agents, workflows, tools, and data.

---

## 1. Ops Automation Studio

- Automates internal operational workflows end-to-end.
- Uses standardized agents for triage, drafting, and routing.
- Built-in approvals and exception handling.
- Strong human-in-the-loop integration.
- Configurable per function or team.
- Reduces cycle time and manual effort.
- Core dependency on workflow orchestration and agent runtime.

---

## 2. Internal Knowledge Assistant

- Question answering over company data.
- Citation-first, permission-aware retrieval.
- Optimized for trust and discoverability.
- Minimal automation complexity.
- Agent usage optional at early stages.
- Suitable as an entry-level factory product.
- Core dependency on knowledge layer.

---

## 3. Reporting Factory

- Generates recurring KPI narratives.
- Detects anomalies and drivers.
- Agent-driven analysis and drafting.
- Evidence-backed outputs.
- Executive-focused delivery.
- Supports scheduled and on-demand runs.
- Heavy agent usage.

---

## 4. Marketing and Docs Factory

- Scalable content and documentation generation.
- Agents handle drafting, rewriting, and structuring.
- Review and approval workflows.
- Brand and style enforcement.
- High reuse of agent templates.
- Supports batch and continuous modes.
- Agent-centric by design.

---

## 5. AI Reliability Suite

- Evaluation, tracing, and red-teaming capabilities.
- Release safety and governance tooling.
- Consumed by other projects.
- Minimal agent usage.
- Platform-level capability.
- Enables enterprise readiness.
- Foundation-heavy, project-light.

---

## 6. Legal Ops Copilot (Clause-to-KPI)

- Contract review and negotiation workflows.
- Agents analyze clauses and detect risk.
- Structured KPI extraction.
- Mandatory human review points.
- Strong policy enforcement.
- Focus on revenue and cycle time.
- Agent-centric.

---

## 7. Voice of Customer Intelligence

- Sentiment, demand, and churn analysis.
- Agents cluster and label themes.
- Evidence-linked insights.
- Continuous ingestion.
- Feeds product and leadership decisions.
- Reusable across industries.
- Agent-centric.

---

## 8. AI Add-On Platform

- AI features embedded into existing software.
- Assistant-style agents.
- Permissioned and traceable actions.
- Safe interaction with product data.
- Gradual rollout support.
- Strong governance.
- Agent-centric.

---

## 9. Codebase Navigator

- Natural-language exploration of repositories.
- Agents map modules and answer questions.
- Citation-based responses.
- Read-only by default.
- Engineering productivity focus.
- High trust requirements.
- Agent-centric.

---

## 10. Factory Control Room

- Central operations dashboard.
- Visibility into workflows and agent runs.
- Cost, quality, and evaluation views.
- Exception and failure management.
- Governance surface.
- Cross-project aggregation.
- Consumes all foundation layers.
